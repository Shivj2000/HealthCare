Arthritis

Arthritis:


It Includes:

  1. Arthritis is one of the most common medical conditions among older people, affecting 10 million people in the UK. It causes joint pain and inflammation which can restrict your movement.
  2. There are two common types of arthritis: osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis. Among older people, osteoarthritis is more common. This is because osteoarthritis is caused by wear and tear; after all, the older we are, the more we have used our joints.
  3. Around eight million people in the UK have this type of arthritis. In contrast, rheumatoid arthritis is an autoimmune disease, where the immune system attacks the lining of the joints.

    4. Symptoms:

    1. Joint pain, tenderness and stiffness.
    2. Restricted movement.
    3. Inflammation in and around the joints.

      4. Treatment:

      1. Hot and Cold Packs
      2. Manual Therapy
      3. Assistive Devices
