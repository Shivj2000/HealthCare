Cancer

Cancer:


It Includes:

  1. Cancer is a disease where cells in the body replicate abnormally and form a mass known as a tumour. These abnormal cells multiply, either causing the tumour to grow or the cancerous cells to spread through the bloodstream.

    2. Symptoms:

    1. Finding an unexpected lump.
    2. Unexplained weight loss.
    3. Unexplained blood in the stool, urine, when coughing, or when vomiting.

      4. Treatment:

      1. Surgery
      2. Radiotherapy
      3. Chemotherapy
